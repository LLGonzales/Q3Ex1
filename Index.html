Villains x ICT

Judge Frollo is a villain featured in the movie "The hunchback of Notre Dame". He is heavily regarded as one of, if not, the greatest villain in Disney. He has a cruel and cunning personality along with a skill in manipulation which lead him to becoming a great villain.

Cedric the Sorcerer is a villain seen in the series "Sofia the First". He is the main villain of the story but is also a sort anti-hero in certain episodes. Nearing the end of the series, he stops becoming a villain.

Shan Yu is the main antagonist of the iconic Disney film "Mulan". He led a large army which successfully breached the Great Wall. He is also a skilled strategist, as seen in how far he got into China and the fact that he still had a large remainder of his army after the battle with the Imperial Army.
